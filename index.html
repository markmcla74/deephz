WARNING / DISCLAIMER

This app displays flashing lights. If you have epilepsy, or someone you live with does, be very careful using this! Use this app at your own risk.

The following people should not attempt brainwave entrainment:

  • Children
  • Pregnant women
  • People with a cardiac pacemaker
  • People who are prone to seizure (i.e. Epilepsy)
